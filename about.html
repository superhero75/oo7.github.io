How it all began

From early 2010, internet censorship has become increasingly prevalent. Traditional VPN technology has been defeated by intrusive government surveillance system and a new wave of VPN has begun to take shape among the open source community to regain internet freedom. The next generation platform has made it possible for us to provide VPN servers completely invisible to the surveillance regimes.

Staying ahead of the curve

Using multi pronged strategy, we strive to use bleeding edge technology for providing absolutely uncompromised safety and privacy on the Web. We will do our best to upgrade to the constantly evolving stealth protocol and stay ahead of our competition with unmatched level of service.
